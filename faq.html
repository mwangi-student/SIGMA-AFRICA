General Info.

Data Science is a multidisciplinary field that involves using scientific methods, algorithms, and systems to extract insights from structured and unstructured data. It's widely applied across industries to drive decision-making and solve complex problems.

Data science encompasses techniques from fields like machine learning, statistics, and data engineering to analyze and interpret data, helping organizations leverage data as a strategic asset.

Data Analytics focuses on examining datasets to draw conclusions about the information they contain. Through techniques like data visualization, statistical analysis, and predictive modeling, it helps businesses make data-driven decisions and improve operational efficiency.

To begin a career in data science, start by building a foundation in programming (Python or R), statistics, and data manipulation techniques. Practical experience with data visualization and machine learning frameworks can also be invaluable.

Consider pursuing relevant certifications, working on personal data projects, and familiarizing yourself with tools like SQL, TensorFlow, and data visualization libraries to strengthen your skill set.
